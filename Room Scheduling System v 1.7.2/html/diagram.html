Logic Diagram 1

Logic diagrams are diagrams in the field of logic, used for representation and to carry out certain types of reasoning.

Data Flow Diagram 2

A data flow diagram (DFD) is a graphical representation of the "flow" of data through an information system, modelling its process aspects. A DFD is often used as a preliminary step to create an overview of the system, which can later be elaborated. DFDs can also be used for the visualization of data processing (structured design).

Entity Relationship Diagram 3

Entity–relationship model (ER model) describes inter-related things of interest in a specific domain of knowledge. An ER model is composed of entity types (which classify the things of interest) and specifies relationships that can exist between instances of those entity types.
